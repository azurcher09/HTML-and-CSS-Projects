The Setting

Warhammer 40,000 is a Sci-Fi/Fantasy setting in which humanity, 40,000 years in the future, has regressed to a state of technological stagnation and must fight for it's survival against a multitude of evil and alien foes.

The Game

This setting is used as the backdrop for a tabletop miniature war game. Players build and paint their models, and pit their army against other players in a battle. There are many factions to choose from.
