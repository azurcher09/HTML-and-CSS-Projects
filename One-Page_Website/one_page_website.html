West Valley Music Academy

Anybody can learn music.

Whether you are a brand new beginner or just trying to get out of a rut, we have a place for you here at West Valley Music Academy.
Our courses are designed to be able to teach anybody to play the instrument they want to.
Take a look at the courses we offer, and let us know where you'd like to start! We would love to have you.

Guitar Basics

A beginner guitar course.
Learn the basics of chords, scales, and more.

Advanced Guitar

Push your guitar skills to the next level.
Learn some of the more exotic chords and scales.

Bass Guitar Basics

A beginner bass guitar course.
Learn the basic scales and how to hold down a groove.

Advanced Bass Guitar

Push your bass skills to the next level.
Learn some of the more advanced bass techniques.

Drumming Basics

A beginner drumming course.
Learn the basics of drumming and how to keep a rhythm while building stamina.

Advanced Drumming

Push your drumming skills to the next level.
Learn some of the more advanced drumming techniques.

Singing

A singing course.
Learn how to sing using your voice's own strengths.

Music Theory Basics

A beginner course on music theory.
Learn how to write music and understand what you play.

Advanced Music Theory

Push your music knowledge to the next level.
Gain more complex and difficult music knowledge.
